Mind Refresher Puzzle!

We chose to tackle the subject of mental health because it can affect everyone.
This issue is on the rise and is still growing, and with the state of mental health services,
this subject seemed like the right one for us. We wanted the user to learn more about mental health,
mental illnessâ€™ impact on the youth, and provide access to resources and information for sake of awareness.
Mental health is a lot like physical health in that many ailments of the mind can be treated and aided with
the proper care, and yet there are still millions who struggle with these issues daily.
We hope to raise awareness on this issue of mental illness, as well as provide a platform for many resources
in order to allow viewers an easy access point to information.

This game is about those with Depression and anxiety. Playing games is a great way to get your friends and family members talking and laughing, and this game can act as a distraction from pain and psychological trauma. Video games can help people who are dealing with mental disorders like anxiety, depression, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), and post-traumatic stress disorder(PTSD).
